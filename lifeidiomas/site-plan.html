Content

Home page

The best experience in your life

We offer the best experiences abroad focused in Asia. This part of the world offers you cultural diversity, exotic food, amazing architecture, nature and a deep historical context.

We understand the travelers

Life Idiomas is a Ecuadorian cultural and travel company that helps people to be merged into Asian culture through exploration and tourism founded by young people that understands the actual needs of the travelers that do not just want luxury but real cultural immersion and saving money.

Professional service

Life idiomas has professional tourist guides who speak English, Spanish, Portuguese, Chinese, Korean and Japanese in order to bring you the best experience customers can imagine and security. Life helps you to travel with confidence.

Images for the Home page

Travel to Korea

More than Kpop

The Republic of Korea is one of the richest and the most technological countries of the world. It is famous for K-Pop music, K-dramas, exotic food, beautiful temples among modern buildings. It has calm and respectful people who really like foreign people.

Discover Korea

Life idiomas guide you through the crowded cities of Seoul and Busan to the amazing beaches in Jeju and more in order to give you an unforgettable experience in the land of your favorite singers and actors.

Dates and price

The tours are organized in Spring and Autumn, the best seasons in Korea. The tour includes flight tickets from your country, housing, transportation, food and activities starting from 6500 USD. The tour is 15 days long to discover and experience Seoul, Incheon, Busan and Jeju Island.

Images for the Page 2 (Travel to Korea)

Travel to Asia

The trip of your life

This is the most adventurous tour we offer. This tour lets you experience China, Singapore, South Korea and Japan in one trip. If you are a fan of Asia and you really want to experiment something different. This is the trip for you.

China

If you want to visit the Chinese Wall, the Forbidden City, enjoy a beautiful time in the street food of China and more this is the trip for you. China is one of the oldest cultures in the world, we have cultural activities and festivals you will not forget in your whole life.

Singapore

This city state is one of the safest countries in the world. This country is a mixture between China, Malaysia and England. You can be able to talk with people in English and make friends. We will visit Singapore Botanic Gardens, Marina Bay Sands and more important places.

South Korea

We will focus on this part of the trip in Seoul. It is the most beautiful and impressive place in South Korea. We will visit NTower, travel by ship through the Han River, eat in the street markets, visit the most important palaces and more.

Japan

We will visit the amazing Tokio and the historical old capital of Kyoto. We will eat the original Sushi, use a Kimono in the old palaces and visit outstanding natural monuments. Even visit Hiroshima.

Dates and price

The tours are organized in Spring and Autumn, the best seasons in Asia. The tour includes flight tickets from your country, housing, transportation, food and activities starting from 12000 USD. The tour is 30 days long to discover and experience Pekin, Hong Kong, Seoul, Tokio, Kyoto and Singapore.
