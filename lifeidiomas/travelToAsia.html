Our pack in brief

If you want to visit the Chinese Wall, the Forbidden City, enjoy a beautiful time in the street food of China and more this is the trip for you.

China

China is one of the oldest cultures in the world, we have cultural activities and festivals you will not forget in your whole life.

You can be able to talk with people in English and make friends. We will visit Singapore Botanic Gardens, Marina Bay Sands and more important places.

Singapore

This city state is one of the safest countries in the world. This country is a mixture between China, Malaysia and England.

We will focus on this part of the trip in Seoul. It is the most beautiful and impressive place in South Korea..

South Korea

We will visit NTower, travel by ship through the Han River, eat in the street markets, visit the most important palaces and more.

Japan

We will visit the amazing Tokio and the historical old capital of Kyoto.
